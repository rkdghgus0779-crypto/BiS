## Topic: Bio and Brain Engineering

Bio and brain engineering is an interdisciplinary field that connects biology, neuroscience, engineering, and data science. Students in this field study biological systems, brain function, neural signals, and computational methods.

## Key Concepts

- Biological data analysis
- Neuroscience and brain function
- Bioinformatics
- Computational modeling
- Machine learning in biology

## Why It Matters

Modern biology increasingly depends on data-driven methods. Learning how to connect biological knowledge with programming can help students understand complex biological systems more deeply.
